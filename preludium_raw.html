Linear Algebra in Orbit-finite Dimension (funded by NCN )

From 2023 I am the principal investigator of the NCN-preludium grant "Linear Algebra in Orbit-finite Dimension" A set is called orbit-finite if it is finite up to the action of some group. The objective of this grant is to study vector spaces generated by orbit-finite sets. The following research works have been supported by this grant.
  • Arka Ghosh, Slawomir Lasota:
    Equivariant ideals of polynomials.
    LICS 2024. (paper) (slides)
  • Michael Cadilhac, Arka Ghosh, Guillermo A. Perez, Ritam Raha:
    Parikh One-Counter Automata.
    Information and Computation. (paper)
  • Shaull Almagor, Arka Ghosh, Tim Leys, Guillermo A. Perez:
    The Geometry of Reachability in Continuous Vector Addition Systems with States.
    Information and Computation. (paper)
  • Arka Ghosh, Piotr Hofman, Slawomir Lasota:
    Orbit-finite Linear Programming.
    Journal of the ACM. (doi) (arxiv version)
