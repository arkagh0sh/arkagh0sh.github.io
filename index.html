I am a PhD student in the Faculty of Mathematics, Informatics and Mechanics of the University of Warsaw. Jointly supervised by Sławek Lasota and Piotrek Hofman.

For my PhD I am working on questions related to model of computations with infinite alphabet, such as register automata and data Petri nets. I am also interested in questions in automata theory and theoretical computer science in general.
